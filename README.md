## Project Overview

CloudBus is a cloud-based online bus pass and ticket booking system designed to eliminate traditional pain points such as ticket loss, theft, incorrect pricing, and system downtime. The platform is built for scalability, reliability, and a seamless user experience.

---

## Features

- **Secure Digital Tickets** — Cryptographically signed QR codes verified at every boarding gate scan
- **Tamper-proof Passes** — Cloud-verified tickets prevent duplication, loss, or theft
- **Dynamic Pricing Lock** — Fare is guaranteed at the time of booking with no last-minute changes
- **Real-time Seat Availability** — Live updates on available seats per departure
- **International Routes** — Supports major European city pairs (London, Paris, Berlin, Madrid, Rome, Amsterdam)
- **Multiple Seat Classes** — Standard, Comfort, and Premium with different amenities
- **Journey Types** — One way, Return, Weekly Pass, Monthly Pass
- **Cloud Infrastructure Dashboard** — Live server load monitoring, uptime display, and auto-scaling indicators
- **PCI DSS Level 1 Payments** — 256-bit AES encryption, card data never stored
- **Booking Summary with QR Pass** — Instant digital pass generated and sent to email on confirmation

## How to Use

1. Select your **From** and **To** city
2. Choose a **departure date** and number of passengers
3. Pick a **seat class** (Standard / Comfort / Premium)
4. Click **Search available departures**
5. Select a departure slot from the results
6. Review the **booking summary** and QR pass
7. Click **Confirm & Pay Securely**
